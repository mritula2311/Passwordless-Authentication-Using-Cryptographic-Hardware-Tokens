How It Works

Simple steps to secure authentication

1
Register

Create your account and register your hardware token or biometric device.

2
Challenge

Server sends a unique cryptographic challenge to your device.

3
Verify

Touch your token, use your fingerprint, or enter your device PIN.

4
Access

Your device signs the challenge and you're securely authenticated.
